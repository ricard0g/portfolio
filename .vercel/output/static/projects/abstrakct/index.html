Abstrakct 🖼️ – Where Art Transcends, and Vision Endures

  • → Dive into the Code on GitHub
  • → Experience Abstrakct Live!

Welcome to Abstrakct – the art gallery where Da Vinci would've dropped his latest masterpiece (if he could figure out WiFi). It's a digital canvas where art isn't just seen, it's felt.

"This isn't just a store. It's an experience. Here, the art feels alive, and every product has a story to tell (and it's not shy about it)."

The idea was to craft a gallery that's more than pixels on a screen. Think buttery-smooth animations, performance that'll make your browser sing, and a style that might just make the Mona Lisa crack a smile.

The Vision Behind The Canvas 🖼️

Building this store was a blast, mostly because it's powered by the Hydrogen Headless Framework. It's like giving your creativity a double espresso shot – all the flexibility, none of the limits.

The goal? Simple: make a gallery that's more than just a pretty face. I wanted buttery-smooth animations, lightning-fast performance, and a style that makes art feel truly alive 🌟.

The Tech Stack Magic Show ⚙️

Brewed With:

Hydrogen Remix Tailwind CSS TypeScript react-spring Netlify

Hydrogen is the backbone, Remix handles routes smoother than a fresh canvas, Tailwind CSS keeps things stylish, TypeScript ensures the code behaves, react-spring adds animations that don't just move (and support SSR!), and Netlify... well, deploying should be easy, but sometimes it just isn't (and yes, there were a few hoops to jump through).
